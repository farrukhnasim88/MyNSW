Key facts about NSW

Nearly a third of all Australians live in New South Wales (NSW), with most living in the state capital of Sydney.
NSW is Australia’s largest state economy.

Capital

Sydney is the capital of New South Wales (NSW). The city of Sydney is located on the eastern coast of NSW

Population

NSW has the highest population of any state in Australia, with 8 million residents as of March 2019.

Roughly 64.5% of the state's population live in Greater Sydney.

NSW has the fastest growing population in Australia growing by roughly 106,100 people annually. Greater Sydney
and Newcastle have the highest population densities in the state, with 391 and 423 people per square kilometre.
